Welcome back! Now, this was supposed to be the SBS (Skaliber's Better Sleeping) Update, as in the first update was the SGT Update, and then the last update was the SMC Update.

Instead of doing the whole SBS update in one go, what I'm gonna do is split the features into two parts, and also throw in a bunch of extra stuff that I've also learned how to do lately.
