Changelog for v0.2

  • Wither Skeletons have a chance to spawn with a Bow
  • Archer Wither Skeletons drop Bows enchanted with Flame
  • Skeletons have a chance to spawn with a Stone Sword
  • Both types of Skeletons only drop Arrows if they are an archer
  • Sheep have a small chance to spawn as any of the colours
  • Mooshrooms drop 1-3 of their respective Mushrooms when they die
    •
  • Improved efficiency of Armour Stand crouch detection system
  • The Prismarine recipe only crafts a single Prismarine Block
  • Re-added 8 Sticks crafting recipe for parity with the Stonecutter recipe
  • Re-added the Stripped Logs from Logs recipe to the Sawmill
  • Ate a Burger
