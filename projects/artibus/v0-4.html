ARTIBUS v0.4 - The Menacing Phantom Update!

Welcome back everybody to Artibus v0.4! In this version we'll see a completely new edition for spawning phantoms into your world!

We'll also take a look at some more interesting tweaks, such as the dynamic between pillagers and lanterns, as well as a bunch of other unique mob mechanics and our first dimension! (totally not over-selling this, okay yeah it's just a void world :/)

  • Chains, end rods, and iron bars can be climbed
