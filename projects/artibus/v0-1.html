ARTIBUS v0.1 - The SGT Update!

Welcome back, nerds. Today we'll be taking a look at the first beta release of Artibus! The way the first few updates are going to work is that we're going to be porting features from my older packs, like SGT, SMC and SBS - which are my old-school datapacks.

Then at some point in the future I'm going to release a full version - v1.0 (probably quite soon), and after that, I'll be working on some much more interesting features like custom dimensions, biomes, structures and mob customisations.

I hope you're interested!

Changelog for v1.0

- The start of a brand new project!
- Added recipe for crafting Clay Block into 4 Clay Balls
- Craft Flower Pots with Nether Bricks
- 3 Snow Blocks give 24 Snow Layers and 3 Snowballs give 6
- Stair recipes give 8 instead of 6
- Prismarine recipes are more consistent
- Sea Lanterns only take 4 Crystals, like Glowstone
- Red Nether Brick crafts like Dark Prismarine
- Magma is crafted with 8 Netherrack and 1 Lava Bucket
- Put Rotten Flesh on a campfire to get Leather
- Cracked Bricks are crafted using a Blast Furnace
- Cobblestone becomes Gravel in a Blast Furnace
- Gravel becomes Sand in a Blast Furnace
- Red Sand becomes Red Stained Glass when smelted
- Sawmill module of Stonecutter - get more wood for your wood!
- Glazed Terracotta is now made in a Stonecutter
- Gravel drops more Flint when mined with an Axe
- Coal Ore has a chance to drop Gunpowder when mined
- Any mob that drops Leather will only drop 1
- Shulkers drop 1-2 Shulker Shells
- Armour Stands have arms, unless you're crouching
- Golden Carrots remove all negative effects when eaten
- Golden Apples give you 'infinite' stacking Absorbtion
- Enchanted Golden Apples are an absolute godsend
